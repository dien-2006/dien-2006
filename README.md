## About Me
I’m a second-year student majoring in Data Engineering at the Posts and Telecommunications Institute of Technology (PTIT) in Hanoi, Vietnam.
I’m especially interested in how data engineering forms the foundation of modern technology — from designing efficient data pipelines to enabling intelligent, data-driven solutions. For me, technology is most meaningful when it transforms raw information into systems that create real-world value.
Alongside my interest in data infrastructure, I’m also passionate about AI and how it can be applied to solve problems in smarter and more scalable ways. I enjoy learning how systems, data, and intelligence work together, and I’m continuously developing my skills through study, practice, and personal projects.

## Goals
- Build a strong foundation in Data Engineering
- Explore AI applications in real-world problems
- Contribute to meaningful technology projects
- Keep learning and growing every day
